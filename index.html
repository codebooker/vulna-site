One durable workflow

From address range to verified fix.

Scope is approved before execution. Evidence arrives as the scan runs. Findings stay connected to context, decisions, tickets, and the verification that closes them.

01 / SCOPE

Authorize

Define owned ranges, select a preset, and issue a signed job that the Scout validates locally.

02 / ASSESS

Observe

Run bounded discovery, vulnerability, TLS, web, or explicitly gated pentest stages.

03 / EXPLAIN

Prioritize

Combine scanner evidence, CVE intelligence, exploit signals, and asset context.

04 / ACT

Remediate

Assign owners, apply SLAs, sync tickets, record decisions, and retain the audit trail.

05 / PROVE

Verify

Run a targeted check against the exact affected surface and preserve the outcome.
